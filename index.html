Biography

Durjoy Mistry is a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Asia Pacific (UAP), Bangladesh. His academic interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Federated Learning, Blockchain, and Computer Vision. Alongside teaching, he is actively involved in research mentorship, academic process automation, and student development initiatives. He also contributes to institutional projects, curriculum enhancement, and co-curricular activities such as club management. As a certified Turnitin instructor, he upholds academic integrity and promotes quality in student work. Durjoy is committed to advancing technology-driven education and fostering a collaborative learning environment at UAP.
